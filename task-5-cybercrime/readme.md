For this task, I researched UPI and online payment fraud, which is one 
of the most common cyber threats faced by students in India. I created 
a case study explaining how scammers use urgency, fake payment 
links, and QR codes to trick users into entering their UPI PIN. I also 
prepared a prevention checklist that included tips such as never 
sharing OTPs, verifying QR codes, and reporting fraud immediately to 
1930 or the National Cyber Crime Portal. 
What surprised me most was how such simple tricks can lead to 
serious financial loss. Many people still believe that entering a UPI 
PIN is necessary to receive money, which scammers misuse. The one 
habit I will personally change is verifying every financial request before 
taking action, especially when urgency is involved. 
This task helped me understand that cyber safety depends on both 
awareness and behavior. Knowing risks is important, but consistently 
applying safe habits is what truly prevents fraud.
