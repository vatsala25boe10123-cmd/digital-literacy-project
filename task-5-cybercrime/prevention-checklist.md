**Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist**

\- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, CVV, or passwords with anyone  
\- Remember: receiving money never requires entering your UPI PIN  
\- Always verify payment requests before approving them  
\- Do not scan QR codes sent by unknown people  
\- Check the sender’s name and UPI ID carefully  
\- Avoid clicking suspicious links in SMS, email, or WhatsApp  
\- Enable app lock and two-factor authentication on banking apps  
\- Use strong and unique passwords for financial apps  
\- For UPI fraud, immediately call 1930  
\- Report the incident on National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
