This task focused on professional communication. I drafted two 
emails: one requesting an assignment extension from a professor and 
another expressing interest in a summer internship. While writing 
these emails, I learned that digital communication is not only about 
sharing information but also about tone, respect, and structure. 
A common problem among students is carrying casual chat habits into 
formal emails. For example, a short message like “Send 
recommendation ASAP” may sound rude even if the request is 
genuine. A better approach is to include a clear subject line, respectful 
greeting, proper explanation, and professional sign-off. 
I also created a social media do’s and don’ts checklist, which 
emphasized responsible posting, privacy, and respectful online 
behavior. This task taught me that professionalism in communication 
directly affects perception, opportunities, and credibility.
